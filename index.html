What is Coinbond (CBD) ?

Coinbond is a decentralized cryptocurrency platform; that provides various financial services based on Blockchain technology, Staking, NFT, P2E, Metaverse, Web3.0 user experience in the crypto scene. The Coinbond token, intended to be a metaverse, NFT and virtual gaming ecosystem, is being shaped around a universe project for investors that is ready to be discovered, unique and built entirely by its users. The CBD token, which opens the door to the Metaverse for its investors with the games it creates, aims to connect the world living in the Metaverse Universe with the tech company it will find.
